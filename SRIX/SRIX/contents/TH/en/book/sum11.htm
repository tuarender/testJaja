Additional information
This is the section in which the candidates might want to provide some extra information. It would be nice if the HR executive gives them a chance to say the things that might not be in the Super Resume format. Some candidates just want to summarize the reasons
why the HR executive should hire them. For example, the candidates' selling points that may win over other candidates.
