Preface
As people are able to move from job to job easily, it creates a unique problem of continuity in terms of company's growth and development. The business edges of focusing on skills of business such as accounting, marketing, finance, and information management will become less and less important. And human resources will never again be the business edges of focusing. The Internet has changed the recruitment process. Excellent recruitment principal is always the first step toward starting each successful company. And digital resume needs to be seriously developed in order to respond to this change. It would be beneficial to have a standardized resume format to apply and hire for employment for any job, anywhere in the world. Such a standardized format would be accepted by job seekers as the best method for representing themselves. Likewise, employers would agree that the standardized format is capable of supplying all necessary information. And Super Resume might be the solution of the world's future standardized digital resume.
 

Not only will the standardized electronic resume change human resources in terms of recruitment, but also change their training and development point of view. Recruitment will change: first, HR executives will come to depend on the newly developed standardized resume format that provides better answers to the new digital recruitment problems. Second, the hiring criteria will focus on three areas: drive, talent and attitude. And in order to meet the future trends in HR development programs, HR will pay more attention to the programs that develop employee skills in applying knowledge and problem solving. And from the Job seekers point of view, they will realize that the current (traditional) resume format has become obsolete. The old resume format will be inconvenient and limited because HR will come to prefer the digital format, through which they will have better information to scan applications and determine who is to be interviewed.

Resume is an issue that relates to everyone because everyone deals with hiring. The online resume makes hiring more efficient and helps everyone make the best employment decisions.

"How to write and analyze a Super Resume" is based on my sixteen years of human resource experience. Our ideas are tested and implemented in the real world: currently Super Resume is the official resume of the top five university job fairs in Thailand. As exposure increases, more and more job seekers and HR executives are relying on the Super Resume. I believe that Super Resume can be world's standardized electronic resume in the near future.

I have been an entrepreneur in Bangkok, Thailand for sixteen years. I am the founder of Topgun Co.,Ltd. My proven working record is to successfully develop Tuk Tuk Ad; a unique moving outdoor advertising medium in Bangkok that is recognized by advertising agencies as the highest reach and frequency medium. I also established the first standardized 4m x 8m billboards network in Thailand. Topgun has been in the advertising and marketing business since 1986. Our rate of growth is derived from our attention to human resources policies and recruitment strategies. We examine a large number of candidates as many as 6,000 for employment each year. Based on this experience we have developed strong recruiting and training systems, as well as organizational activities, to find the best employees and provide them with the best quality of life possible.
